About

Hello! My name is Jovin Kyenkungu. I am currently a part-time fullstack iOS developer and also a recent graduate with a Computer Science degree from the University of Toronto. As a fullstack iOS developer, I am well versed in Swift and Obj-C as well as NodeJS and NoSQL My interests also lay in web development, working mainly with React, Go and AWS on personal projects. Tint

Tint

Tint is a simple and intuitive cross-platform to-do list application inspired by the pervasive swipe page navigation style and personal flat UI desgins. Made with ReactNative, the app allows one to hand/style draw notations and provides plesant colour changes to the UI as visual cues relative to the amount of tasks one has. As a personal project, it is still in production with no intention of being published on the App stores.

Course Flex

Course Flex

A group of three computer science students and I decided to develop a more interactive and intuitive university course searching website. Course Flex's goal was to provide a user-friendly website for UofT students to explore courses with ease by catering to their interests, optimizing the search function and allowing students to post ratings and reviews.

About

As a fullstack developer, I am well versed in JS, NoSQL, SQL and React and whilst my preference to develop for android arises from my love of Java, the broad school curriculum has taught me to be comfortabled with other languages such as C and Haskell. Projects

Course Flex

Course Flex

For a semester long project required by CSC309: Developing On The Web, Me plus a group of three computer science students decided to develop a more interactive and intuitive university course searching website. Course Flex's goal was to provide a user-friendly website for UofT students to explore courses with ease by catering to their interests, optimizing the search function and allowing students to post ratings and reviews. The course required all of us to be active in all stages of development, implementing JQuery for the frontend and, Node with MongoDB for our Backend.

Amani

Wear Hacks

WearHacks

As a member of StallionEye, we participated in WearHacks Montreal as an opportunity to examine and discover new technologies. Developing with Estimote, we created a native iOS application that enables user locations to be tracked in an allocated space by another user, targeting teachers and parents. Delegated to do the backend, I worked on bluetooth connectivity and position tracking on Swift.
